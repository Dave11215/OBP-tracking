Prehab calendar

Running sessions

Easy Run + strides — Zone 2

30–40 min conversational (HR ~125–140, cap 145), then 4–6 x 10–15s strides. Keep it genuinely easy.

Quality Run — midweek hard (gated)

5K tempo / threshold. Warm up, finish strong but not to failure, cadence high. Swap to Bike VO2 if legs/HRV need it.

Track intervals — speed (gated)

WU 12–15 min + drills, then 5–6 x 400–800 m at ~5K effort (HR to upper Z4, cap ~172) with generous recovery, CD. Never a time trial.

Long Run — weekend endurance

Easy-to-moderate, progressing ~a mile a week (~4 → 5 → 6 → 6–7). Outdoors on the weekend.

Bike sessions

Zone 2 Bike — easy base, joint-sparing

Conversational Zone 2, HR 120–140 (soft cap 145), 45–90 min. Long outdoor ride on the weekend; carbs + salted fluid past ~75 min.

Bike VO2 — impact-free hard

WU 10–12 min, intervals at ~90–95% max HR (3–4 min on / 3–4 easy), CD. Redline is fine, no impact. Never to failure. This is where the over-threshold work lives.

What we track, and why

Track towards
  • Vigorous minutes, chronic (28-day)The dose that matters is chronic, not acute. One hard session does not move it; a season of them does.
  • Vigorous days per weekCountable. Garmin computes it natively over a rolling 6-week window.
  • Strength sessions per weekStrength and aerobic together beat either alone: HR 0.60, vs 0.71 aerobic-only and 0.89 strength-only. The dose optimum is about 40 min/week, so the 2x floor is already right. Do not inflate it. The real risk is letting cardio crowd it out.
  • Resting HR, trendRR 1.09 per 10 bpm across 1.2M people. Better evidenced than HRV. Read the trend, never the level.
  • Sleep durationShort sleep in midlife carries real risk. This is the window where it counts.
Govern by (monitor, do not target)
  • HRV, overnight, raw msA governor, not a goal. It answers "go hard today?" It is not a scoreboard. Use the raw number, not the status label.
  • Sleep continuityAwakenings and restless moments.
  • Acute:chronic load ratioOverreach management, not a health metric.
